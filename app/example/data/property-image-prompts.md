## Usage Notes

- All prompts are designed for 4:3 aspect ratio (800x600px recommended)
- Adjust prompts based on your AI model's requirements (some models prefer shorter prompts, others work better with detailed descriptions)
- For properties marked "Off Plan", you may want to emphasize "architectural rendering" or "visualization" in the prompt
- For "Ready to Move" properties, emphasize "professional real estate photography" and realistic details
- Consider adding style modifiers like "photorealistic", "high quality", "professional photography" based on your AI model
- For interior shots, you can add specific room types (living room, kitchen, bedroom, bathroom) to the prompt
- For exterior shots, consider adding time of day (golden hour, daylight, evening) for variety
